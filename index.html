About Us

Our Mission

At Batam Clean City Initiative, we are on a mission to make our city a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable place to live. We believe that a clean city not only enhances the quality of life for its residents but also sets an example for future generations.

Who We Are

We are a passionate group of individuals, volunteers, and environmental enthusiasts who have come together to address the growing concerns of pollution, waste, and environmental degradation in our beloved city. With a shared commitment to the environment and a desire to create lasting change, we've embarked on this journey to make a difference.
